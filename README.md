# Dad vs Argus 💨

A two-player same-keyboard chase platformer where a father and son settle their differences the only way nature intended — by jumping on each other and letting loose.

Best of 3 rounds. May the gassiest player win.

## How to play

Two players, one keyboard.

| | Dad | Argus |
|---|---|---|
| Move | A / D | ← / → |
| Jump | W | ↑ |
| Damage | 15/hit | 10/hit |
| Speed | Slower | Faster |

**Jump on the other player to fart on them.** Only the player above deals damage — so get the high ground and let gravity do the rest.

First to 2 round wins takes the match.

## Tune it

Open the settings panel on the start screen to tweak gravity, jump height, speed, damage, and friction in real time. Your settings are saved automatically.
